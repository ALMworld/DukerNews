03
How It Works

The DUKI Flow

Every payment on DukerNews — mint, post, boost — follows this flow.

Maker shares profits globally

Beyond just marketing spend going to ad platforms, makers commit a percentage of revenue to the DUKI treasury — open, on-chain, verifiable.

Taker completes the deal

When a taker buys, stablecoin flows into the treasury. Two tokens are minted: DUKI (locked, backs real income) and ALM (50% maker · 50% taker — governance).

DUKI flows to all verified humans

Distributed proportionally via World ID. No bots. No whales. Every real person on Earth receives their share of kindness income.

Spend or redeem freely

Use DUKI as stablecoin, or redeem for underlying stablecoins from the treasury. The world's future depends on all.
